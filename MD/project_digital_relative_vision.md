**Concrete proof point — the email mess (added 2026-04-24, same conversation):**
The 2030 vision is abstract until you name a real-world hurt. The hurt: most users have multiple email accounts (Elijah's case: Gmail + Outlook + AOL). To clean their own inbox of spam they have to grant a third-party tool (Cleanfox, Mailstrom, Unroll.me) READ access to ALL of them. That's now FOUR companies handling personal email data so the user can do basic janitorial work in their own house. Plus the providers themselves train their own AI on that mail.

The Master AI version: your local brain reads YOUR email, unsubscribes you, summarizes what's important, deletes the noise — no third party, no data exfil, no permission asked from a vendor. THAT is what "digital relative who protects us and our information" means in concrete terms.

**How to use this proof point:**
- In the pitch: lead with the email-mess story before the 2030 vision. The hurt sells the destination.
- Common variations of the same proof point: photo storage (Apple/Google/Dropbox all hold copies), banking statements (each bank's app, scattered), receipts/warranties (lost in inboxes), medical records (provider portals). All same shape: user's own data scattered across vendors who profit from it. Master AI consolidates it back under the user.
- Quote-ready line (Elijah's own framing): "three accounts, four companies, none of them yours. That's the hurt. Master AI is the answer."
- This proof point is the bridge from "abstract vision" to "I want this." People don't move on visions; they move on stories of what hurts.

**Email is just the entry point — the bigger frame (added same day):**
Email-handling is the doorway to the larger digital-relative role: the AI as your gatekeeper for incoming information. Beyond unsubscribe:
- **Job alerts**: surfaces postings that match what you're actually looking for, drops the noise.
- **Shopping deals + coupons**: catches deals on items you've actually mentioned wanting — not what an algorithm thinks you might want.
- **Business correspondence**: separates real action items from CC chains and reply-all noise.
- **Subscriptions + bills**: alerts before charges hit, summarizes what changed since last statement.
- **Routine communication**: drafts replies in your voice for routine stuff (appointment confirmations, RSVPs, thank-yous), waits for your nod before sending.

Same principle every time: the AI surfaces what matters TO YOU, handles the routine, asks before anything irreversible. None of it requires a third-party vendor reading your mail. This is what "digital relative" means in operational terms — a household member who handles your incoming mail, post, and notifications the way a thoughtful family member would, but at machine scale and with your privacy intact.
