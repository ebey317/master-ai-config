## Elijah's pitch, 2026-04-21 (his words, preserved):

*"I have all these thoughts and ideas and I don't know where to start and that is where you turn to Master AI. If I was to make that into a commercial, I would say like ask Alexa or tapping into a Google Chrome and you say I've got all these ideas and I don't know where to start help me out and then the whole Master AI logo just scroll down like it would in the program or even the [Claude] scroll down like it did in a program and it would naturally enter in a plan mode and we watch it switch and develop a plan — not of what he has but based off of that when you have a lot of thoughts and ideas and you don't know where to go step one write things down, categorize, you know things like that whatever it would actually do. I think that's the best pitch for this."*

## Why it works
- **Universal pain point.** Every buyer has felt "too many ideas, don't know where to start." Doesn't require product knowledge to feel.
- **Shows the product, not features.** Plan mode drafting in real time IS the proof. No voiceover listing features.
- **Differentiates from Alexa/ChatGPT.** One-shot Q&A tools can't do this. Structured iterative planning is Master AI's lane.
- **Respects the viewer.** No hype music, no superlatives — the product earns it on screen.

## Claude's refinements (keep or reject as you like)
- **Use one concrete example, not abstract.** "Help me start Sunkissed Soul for under $500" or "help me plan my daughter's birthday" — something real. Abstract asks land generic; specific asks land personal.
- **Handshake moment is the hero shot**, not the logo scroll. When the frame repaints red → amber at the `1/Enter` press, that's the visual signature. "The moment the plan becomes action." Close the spot on that.
- **Differentiator line:** *"Alexa answers. Master AI plans."* Three words, names the category gap, no negative competitor mention.

## Format guesses (not locked)
- 30-second spot, one arc, one ask, one plan-ready moment, one handshake.
- Or: 15-second social cut showing just the user's line → the handshake flip → "Master AI" end card.
- Long-form (60s) adds the user executing the plan and it actually working — proof of follow-through.

## How to apply
- When Elijah opens marketing work, this is the canonical pitch. Start here, don't re-invent.
- Don't water it down for "enterprise" audiences. The ache is universal; speak to humans, not companies.
- The product IS the ad. Show the real UI. No mockups.
